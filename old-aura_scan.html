What is an Aura Scan?

An Aura Scan is a gentle, intuitive way to explore your animal's emotional and energetic well-being. Through this compassionate process, I tune into your companion’s energy field to sense areas of stress, imbalance, or sensitivity.

Every living being radiates energy — and your animal companion is no exception. Their aura, the subtle energy field surrounding their body, reflects their emotional state, physical health, and spiritual vitality.

When this field is balanced, your pet feels calm, grounded, and at peace. But when imbalances occur due to stress, illness, trauma, or environmental changes, they may manifest as behavioral shifts, anxiety, or even physical discomfort.

Through an Aura Scan, I gently tune into your animal’s energy and identify areas that may need attention, support, or healing. This process helps you understand what your companion is experiencing on a deeper level — beyond what behavior alone can express.

Benefits of an Aura Scan

  • Identify emotional stress or anxiety your pet may be holding
  • Detect energetic imbalances before they manifest physically
  • Gain clarity on behavioral changes or unexplained fears
  • Support your animal's overall emotional and spiritual wellness
  • Deepen your understanding of your companion’s inner world

What to Expect

  • A detailed written report describing your animal's aura field
  • Insights into emotional patterns, stress areas, and energetic health
  • Recommendations for supporting balance and well-being
  • Up to 3 follow-up healing sessions included in the price
